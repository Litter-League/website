Who We Are

About the
Litter League

A science-based community outreach organization working to end plastic pollution in both built and natural environments.

Our Mission at a Glance

Our Mission

Education, Research & Outreach to End Plastic Pollution in the Environment

Our Goals

  • Increase education and awareness of plastic pollution to all communities
  • Reduce litter in the environment
  • Prevent new litter from entering the environment
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to inform evidence- and science-based policy and management
  • Share lessons learned openly and widely, inspiring others worldwide
  • Provide training and leadership opportunities to students and community members

Our Vision

A world free of macro- and microplastics in the environment. Until then, we envision an engaged community working to prevent and reduce litter in their environment.
