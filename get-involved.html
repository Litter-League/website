How to Help

Pick Your Superpower

Get Outside & Clean Up
01

Volunteer at a Cleanup

This is the heart of what we do. Show up, grab a bag and a grabber, and spend a couple of hours making your neighbourhood measurably cleaner. No experience required — just enthusiasm.

  • Events every weekend in multiple locations
  • All equipment provided — just wear closed-toe shoes
  • Kid- and dog-friendly events available
  • Track your personal impact with our volunteer app
Sign Up to Volunteer
🏫
Bring the League to School
02

School & Youth Programs

We bring our litter education curriculum to schools, summer camps, and youth organizations. Teachers and youth group leaders can request a free workshop or schoolyard cleanup event.

  • Free in-class workshops for K–12 classes
  • Litter mapping projects for science classes
  • Schoolyard cleanup events with student data collection
  • Curriculum aligned with environmental science standards
Request a School Program
⭐
Lead Your Community
03

Become a Block Captain

Ready to level up? Block Captains are community leaders who organize their own local cleanups under the Litter League banner, with our full support — supplies, training, and promotion.

  • We provide all supplies and equipment
  • Full training and onboarding support
  • Digital tools to recruit and manage volunteers
  • Featured on our website and social channels
