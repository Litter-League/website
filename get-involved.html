How to Help

Upcoming Activities

🎓

Education & Awareness

Increasing education and awareness of plastic pollution across all communities through workshops, school programs, and outreach.

🧹

Litter Reduction

Cleaning up litter from the environment through litter cleanups and trash trapping in the future in parks, waterways, and our communities.

🌿

Pollution Prevention

Working to stop new litter from entering the environment in the first place, through behaviour change and community engagement.

🔬

Science-Based Policy

Collaborating with stakeholders to inform evidence- and science-based environmental policy and management decisions.

🌍

Global Knowledge Sharing

Publishing our data and lessons learned openly and widely — inspiring communities around the world to take action.

🌱

Training & Leadership

Providing hands-on training and leadership opportunities to all Litter League members.

🪄

Got Another Idea?

Think there's a way to contribute that we haven't thought of yet? We're all ears. Reach out and let's figure out how your skills fit into the Litter League.
