What We Do

Fighting litter on every front

🧹

Community Cleanups

We organize regular volunteer cleanup events in parks, streets, and shorelines — making it easy for anyone to show up and make a difference.

🎓

Youth Education

Through school programs and workshops, we teach the next generation about litter's impact on ecosystems, wildlife, and public health.

📊

Litter Data & Research

We collect and analyze litter data from every cleanup to identify hotspots, track trends, and inform targeted intervention strategies.

🏛️

Policy Advocacy

We work with local governments to push for stronger anti-littering ordinances, better waste infrastructure, and producer responsibility legislation.
